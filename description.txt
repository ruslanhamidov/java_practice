Logic of coffeshop

Work Flow:
Order* is given to Cashier* which processes order payment and adds it to CashRegister* and Barista* prepares the order

Order -> Cashier -> CashRegister -> Product -> Barista

Order is object with Product(product_name, product_price) and payment
Product is object with price and name

Cashier serves as bridge between Order and CashRegister. Cashier gets Price and Payment and writes it to CashRegister.
